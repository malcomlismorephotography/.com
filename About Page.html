About Malcolm Lismore

I am Malcom Lismore, a photographer currently based on the North West coast of Scotland. With over a decade of experience, I have sharpened my skills in capturing the rugged beauty of the Scottish landscapes and the essence of natural wildlife. Through my lens, I aim to create timeless and captivating images that tell stories and evoke emotions.

My passion for photography has taken me on numerous adventures, allowing me to showcase the raw and untouched beauty of Scotland's natural wonders.
